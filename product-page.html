Air Jordan 11 "Varsity Red"

Stay in the loop for future releases

The Upper

The upper of the silhouette’s signature shiny patent leather mudguard arrives in a “cherry” red hue, wrapping around the base of the shoe.

Extending from the mudguards is a white mesh and leather upper with matching colored eyelets and laces.

Accents

On the back heel, a varsity red Jumpman is embroidered on the white leather with Jordan’s number 23, embroidered in white on the varsity red overlay

On the tongue, an embroidered strip features “JUMPMAN JORDAN”

Cushioned Sole

A frosted, semi-translucent rubber outsole adds an icy touch to the Air Jordan 11 Varsity Red, paired with a white midsole, which also matches the upper.
